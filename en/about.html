About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Lilian, short for Elisabeth-Ann.

Through this website, I share my thoughts, projects, and my passion for creativity and nature.

I am a state-certified educator with a bachelor's degree and trained experiential educator. For many years, I worked passionately in various kindergartens and gained valuable experience.

I was especially inspired by one setting where everything harmonized beautifully. There, I began writing stories for children – small dream journeys that stimulate imagination and bring joy. It was indescribably beautiful to see the children’s eyes light up as I read my stories aloud. That moment showed me the special power of my words.

Even earlier, I wrote passionately, but only now have I found the courage to share my stories with the world. That’s how the first volumes of my "Dream Journeys with Baldur" came to life. Volumes 1 and 2 are now available, and I’ve also dared to write a book for adults, which has also been published.

To reach even more people with my stories, I will offer audio versions here and on YouTube.

I publish my books independently, supported by my husband's technical know-how. It’s an exciting journey, and I’m looking forward to creating many more works.

But writing is not my only passion – for years, I’ve been fascinated by the world of herbs. I make my own natural cosmetics and have discovered how valuable and effective chemical-free alternatives are. I’m happy to share these experiences by publishing my recipes for skincare, tea blends, salves, and herbal remedies here.

Whether it’s calming teas, natural care products, or homemade remedies – I love using the power of nature to help others.

What matters to me:

  • Music
  • Yoga
  • A mindful and conscious approach to nature
  • Chemical-free alternatives for cosmetics, hygiene products, food, and medicine
  • Everything related to relaxation and inner balance
  • Free expression and transformation through words
